PUMA MUSE ECHO

28.11.2017 | PUMA

An all-new progressive sneaker that takes design cues from both the world of martial arts and ballet, we introduce to you the MUSE ECHO (R1 999). Fusing the contrasts of geometric shapes and exaggerated proportions with feminine, soft, and lightweight materials. The unapologetic and truly badass trainer is performance-inspired and fashion-forward. The woven upper sits atop a daring, split outsole for a progressive look.
